# Team Assignment

You can assign a managed account to one or more teams. When a team has shared task categories, those categories become available in the dependant's task tree after assignment.

## Assigning a team

1. Go to **Dependants** (`/managed-accounts`) and expand the account.
2. Scroll to the **Teams** section.
3. Select a team from the dropdown and click **Assign**.

The team's shared categories are now available in the dependant's Organize page and timer picker.

## Removing a team assignment

In the Teams section, click the **remove** button next to a team. The team's shared categories are removed from the dependant's view.

## Viewing team dependants

Team creators can see all managed accounts assigned to their team on the **Teams** page under the Dependants section of the team detail panel.

> Only the **owner** of a managed account can assign or remove teams.

## Invite emails and managed accounts

When a new member is invited to a team that cares for one or more managed accounts, the invite email lists the names of those dependants. This lets the invitee know in advance whose accounts they will be able to access before accepting.
