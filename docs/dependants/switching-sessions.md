# Switching Sessions

To track or review time for a managed account, you switch into their session. While in a dependant session, the entire app operates as that account — you see their tasks, entries, goals, and reports.

![Session switch banner](/img/screenshots/session-banner.png)

## Switching from the home page

On the home page (`/`), the **Dependant timers** panel shows all your managed accounts with currently running timers. Click **Switch** on any row to enter that account's session.

## Switching from the Dependants page

1. Go to **Dependants** (`/managed-accounts`).
2. Expand the account you want to access.
3. Click **Switch to this account**.

## Switching from the user menu

Click your avatar in the top-right corner. Any managed accounts you have access to appear in the menu. Click one to switch.

## While in a dependant session

- A banner at the top of every page shows the dependant's name.
- All actions (starting timers, editing entries, etc.) affect the dependant's data, not yours.
- The nav and all features work exactly as they do for a regular account.

## Returning to your account

Click the **Return** button in the top banner, or open the user menu and select your own name.
