# Member Roles

Each team member is assigned one of two roles. The role controls what they can see and do within the team.

## Roles

### Contributor

- Can track time against task categories shared with the team.
- Cannot view other members' time entries in Reports.
- Cannot share categories (only owners can share).

### Collaborator

- Everything a Contributor can do, plus:
- Can view and filter by other team members' time data in the [Reports](../reports/filters.md) page.

## Changing a member's role

Only the team **creator** can change roles.

1. Go to **Teams** (`/teams`) and select the team.
2. In the members list, click the role badge next to a member's name.
3. Select the new role from the dropdown.

## Pending members

Invited members show a **Pending** badge until they accept the invitation. Pending members cannot access any team features.
