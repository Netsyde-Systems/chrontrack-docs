# Profile & Security

The **Profile** page (`/profile`) lets you update your display name and change your password.

![Profile page](/img/screenshots/profile.png)

## Changing your display name

1. Go to **Profile** (`/profile`) via the user menu.
2. Edit the **Name** field.
3. Click **Save**. A confirmation message appears briefly.

Your display name appears in the navigation bar, on team member lists, and in shared-category owner labels.

## Changing your password

> The password section is only shown if your account uses **email/password** sign-in. Social login accounts (Google, Facebook, Microsoft) do not have a Chrontrack password.

1. Enter your **current password**.
2. Enter your **new password** (minimum 8 characters).
3. Re-enter the new password in the **Confirm** field.
4. Click **Update password**.

For security, you are signed out of all other sessions when your password changes.

## Email address

Your email address is shown read-only on the Profile page. It cannot be changed here — it is set by the authentication provider you used to sign up.
