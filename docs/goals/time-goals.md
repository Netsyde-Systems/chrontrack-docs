# Goals

Goals let you set time targets for any task and period, then track your progress against them.

![Goals page](/img/screenshots/goals.png)

## Goal types

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **At least** | You want to spend a minimum amount of time (e.g. exercise at least 5 h/week) |
| **At most** | You want to cap time spent (e.g. TV at most 10 h/week) |

## Periods

Goals can be set for any of these periods:

- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Annual
- All time

## Creating a goal

1. Go to **Goals** (`/goals`).
2. Click **New goal**.
3. Select the **task** using the task picker.
4. Choose the **period**.
5. Choose the **type** (At least / At most).
6. Enter the **target** in hours and minutes.
7. Click **Save**.

![New goal form](/img/screenshots/goal-form.png)

## Progress indicators

Each goal card shows a progress bar colored by status:

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green | On track / achieved |
| Yellow | At risk |
| Red | Over (for "at most" goals) or behind (for "at least" goals) |

The card also shows the time logged so far in the current period and the target.

## Editing a goal

Hover over a goal card and click the **edit** icon to reopen the form with current values.

## Deleting a goal

Hover over a goal card and click the **delete** icon, then confirm.

## Goal grouping

Goals are grouped by period on the page (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, etc.) for easy scanning.
