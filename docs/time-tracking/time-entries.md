# Time Entries

The time entry list, shown below the timer on the home page, displays your recent sessions grouped by day.

![Time entry list](/img/screenshots/time-entries.png)

## Reading an entry

Each entry shows:

- **Task path** — the full path through your hierarchy (e.g. Professional › Work)
- **Tags** — any tags applied to the session
- **Duration** — how long the session lasted
- **Start and end time**

## Editing an entry

Click the **edit** (pencil) icon on any entry to open the edit form. You can change:

- The associated task
- The tags
- The start and end time (and therefore the duration)

Click **Save** to apply your changes.

## Deleting an entry

Click the **delete** (trash) icon on an entry and confirm the prompt to remove it permanently.

## Live entries

If a timer is currently running, its entry appears at the top of the list with a live-updating duration. It cannot be edited until the timer is stopped.
