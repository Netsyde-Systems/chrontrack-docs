# Authentication

Chrontrack supports several sign-in methods. All methods land you in the same app with the same features.

## Sign-in options

| Method | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Google** | One-click OAuth via your Google account |
| **Facebook** | One-click OAuth via your Facebook account |
| **Microsoft** | One-click OAuth via your Microsoft account |
| **Email & password** | Traditional sign-up / sign-in with any email address |

![Login page](/img/screenshots/login.png)

## Signing in with a social provider

1. Open the Chrontrack login page.
2. Click the **Google**, **Facebook**, or **Microsoft** button.
3. Complete the OAuth flow in the pop-up window.
4. You are redirected to the app automatically.

## Signing in with email and password

1. Enter your **email address**.
2. Enter your **password**.
3. Click **Sign in**.

If you don't have an account yet, switch to the **Sign up** tab, fill in your name, email, and a password (minimum 8 characters), then click **Create account**.

After signing up, ChronTrack sends a **confirmation email** to the address you provided. Click the link in that email to verify your account. The app is accessible before you confirm, but some features may prompt you to verify first.

## Welcome emails

All new accounts receive a welcome email:

- **Email & password accounts** — receive a combined welcome and email confirmation email. Click the link inside to verify your address.
- **Social sign-in accounts** (Google, Facebook, Microsoft) — receive a welcome email with no action required. Your email address is already verified by the social provider.

## Signing out

Click your avatar in the top-right corner and select **Sign out**.
