# Sharing Categories

You can share any task category (and its subtree) with a team. Team members can then **mount** the shared category into their own task tree, track time against it, and see its entries in reports.

This is useful for organizations that want a consistent task structure across all members without requiring everyone to maintain their own copy.

![Share modal](/img/screenshots/share-modal.png)

## Sharing a category

1. Go to **Organize** (`/organize`).
2. Find the task node you want to share and click the **Share** button on its row.
3. In the share modal, choose the **team** to share with from the dropdown.
   - If the team doesn't exist yet, click **Create new team** to create it inline.
4. Click **Share**. The team now appears in the "Shared with" list.

> Only the **owner** of a category can share it.

## Revoking a share

1. Open the share modal for the category (Share button on the task row).
2. Click the **remove** (×) button next to the team you want to revoke.
3. Confirm the prompt.

Team members who had the category mounted will see it marked as unavailable.

## Receiving shared categories

When someone shares a category with a team you belong to, it appears in the **Shared with me** section at the bottom of the Organize page.

See [Receiving Shares](../teams/receiving-shares.md) for instructions on mounting and unmounting shared categories.
