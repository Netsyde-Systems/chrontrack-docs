# Offline support

ChronTrack is built to work without an internet connection. Your data lives in a local database in your browser, so you can log time, check your entries, and review goals even when you're offline.

## Sync indicator

A small indicator in the navigation bar shows the current sync state:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green dot | All changes are synced to the server |
| Amber / pulsing | Sync in progress |
| Red / disconnected | Offline — changes are saved locally |

## What works offline

Everything in the main app works offline:

- Starting and stopping the timer
- Viewing and editing time entries
- Browsing and editing your task hierarchy (Organize page)
- Checking goals and progress
- Reading reports for already-loaded data

## What requires a connection

A few actions need the server:

- Signing in or signing out
- Inviting team members or accepting invitations
- Creating or managing managed (dependant) accounts

## Coming back online

When your connection is restored, ChronTrack automatically syncs any changes you made offline. You don't need to refresh the page or take any action — the sync indicator will turn green once everything is up to date.
